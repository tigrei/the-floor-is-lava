🌍 The Modern Context

Lessons from Ukraine

We see the importance of logistics today in Ukraine, where the Ukrainian military is advancing on multiple fronts all backed by attacks on logistic supply chains.


The Pacific Theater

We know in a conflict scenario within the Strait of Taiwan and the South China Sea, logistics will be absolutely paramount in a First Island Chain defense.

🗺️ 1. SITUATION

You are in command of supply and delivery operations for expeditionary forces in the contested First Island Chain.

Under intense Gray Zone pressure, neighboring ports depend on your logistics support to maintain deterrence.

🎯 2. MISSION OBJECTIVES

  • Load Cargo: Pick up vital construction materials, communication grids, mobile shelters, and heavy machinery at Base Ports.
  • Route Optimization: Deliver requests to contested sites before deadlines expire.
  • Prevent Domino Effect: If ports remain unsupplied in a high-need status, their urgency escalates. Critical ports will roll to become CONTESTED, blocking sea lanes until neighboring base nodes facilitate a recovery.

🚢 Simulation Engine & Realism

  • Authentic Operations: Uses real naval routes and maritime scenarios, including resource constraints and supply deficits.
  • Dynamic Scenarios: Generates realistic cargo delivery missions with authentic timelines and operational limits.
  • Training Value: Trains players to make high-stakes logistical decisions where efficiency, risk management, and timing directly determine mission success.

📡 Architecture & Data Modeling

  • Data Inputs: JSON-based structures that can be easily modified to run off modern REST API inputs.
  • Weather Data Modeling: A matrix ocean simulation with 10 evolving weather states (ranging from calm to typhoons) driven by wind, waves, and swell.
  • Lightweight Deployment: 100% vanilla browser-based deployment; no installation barriers.
